Sedudo Waterfall

Sedudo Waterfall is one of the popular tourist attractions in Nganjuk. it is part of culture in the city, every year theres a rituals that mark the javanese new year that celebrated during the month known as Sura, sometimes written as Suro.

Semantok Dam

Semantok Dam is opened by the 7th president Joko Widodo 2022. it is the longest dam in south east asia. it mainly uses for irigation but it also serves as tourist attraction.

Jolotundo Edupark

Jolotundo Edupark is relatively new tourist attraction that open in Nganjuk. It offer glamping experience, it also have mini zoo. It also perfect palce for family with kid, to teach about nature.
